Résumé

Current & Recent Projects

1998–present
Film Critic, Variety, Los Angeles — Toronto (1998) and Berlin (1999) film festivals
1998–present
Catalogue Writer, Hamptons International Film Festival, East Hampton, NY
1997–present
Catalogue Writer, Sydney Film Festival, Sydney, Australia
1996–present
Contributing Editor & Film Critic, Nitrate Online
1997–present
Writer/Presenter, The Smithsonian Associates, Washington, DC — Understanding the Art of Film, Comedy Today, and others
1996–present
Catalogue Writer, The Philadelphia Festival of World Cinema
1994–present
Guest Presenter, Key Biograph Theaters (Washington, DC) and Charles Theater (Baltimore, MD) — Sunday sneak preview programs
1992–present
Executive Director, The Americas Film Festival Foundation, Washington, DC — annual festival of Spanish and Portuguese film and video
1988–present
Senior Catalogue Editor, Programmer & Presenter, Filmfest DC: The Washington, DC International Film Festival
1994–1995
Film Critic, Fox Morning News / Fox News at Noon, Washington, DC
1990–1993
Adjunct Faculty, The American University, Washington, DC — History of Motion Pictures, The Crime Thriller

Employment History

1977–1982, 1986–1992
Associate Director, Film Programming, The American Film Institute National Exhibition Programs, The Kennedy Center, Washington, DC
1990–1992
Curator, Western European Sidebar, The American Film Institute National Video Festival, Los Angeles
1987–1996
Consultant, AFI L.A. FilmFest, Los Angeles — programming and film notes
1984–1985
Columnist, Film Talk, The Washington Post — local and world film news in the Friday Weekend section
1983–1984
Film Critic, The Washington Tribune
1982–1985
Film Specialist, United States Information Agency Exhibits Service — created and traveled with touring exhibit Filmmaking in America
1979–1989
Writer/Host, Insights on Film, WGMS Radio, Rockville, MD

Education

1978
B.A., Cinema Studies/Literature, The American University, Washington, DC — Cassell Award, Outstanding Overall Achievement
