Online Film Critics Society 1998 Awards

Feature by Harvey Karten◆Published 1 January 1999

Online Film Critics Society
1998 Awards

The 142-member Online Film Critics Society is composed of critics whose primary affiliations are internet web sites and online services.

Best Film

  • Saving Private Ryan

Also Nominated (in order)

  • The Truman Show
  • Life is Beautiful
  • Pleasantville
  • Shakespeare in Love
  • The Thin Red Line
  • Happiness
  • The Butcher Boy
  • The Big Lebowski
  • Out of Sight

Best Actor

  • Ian Mc Kellen (Gods and Monsters)

Also Nominated

  • Tom Hanks (Saving Private Ryan)
  • Edward Norton (American History X)

Best Actress

  • Cate Blanchett (Elizabeth)

Also Nominated

  • Gwyneth Paltrow (Shakespeare in Love)
  • Emily Watson (Hilary and Jackie)

Best Supporting Actor

  • Billy Bob Thorton (A Simple Plan)

Also Nominated

  • Ed Harris (The Truman Show)
  • Jason Patric (Your Friends and Neighbors)

Best Supporting Actress

  • Joan Allen (Pleasantville)

Also Nominated

  • Kathy Bates (Primary Colors);
  • Lisa Kudrow (The Opposite of Sex)

Best Ensemble

  • Saving Private Ryan

Also Nominated

  • Happiness
  • Your Friends and Neighbors

Best Director

  • Steve Spielberg (Saving Private Ryan)

Also Nominated

  • Terrence Malick (The Thin Red Line)
  • Peter Weir (The Truman Show)

Best Original Screenplay

  • The Truman Show (Andrew Niccol)

Also Nominated

  • Happiness (Todd Solondz)
  • Shakespeare in Love (Marc Norman and Tom Stoppard)

 Best Adapted Screenplay

  • Out of Sight (Scott Frank)

Also Nominated

  • Primary Colors (Elaine May)
  • A Simple Plan (Scott B. Smith)

Best Original Score

  • Pleasantville (Randy Newman)

Also Nominated

  • The Prince of Egypt (Hans Zimmer)
  • Saving Private Ryan (John Williams)

Best Cinematography

  • Saving Private Ryan (Janusz Kaminski)

Also Nominated

  • Pleasantville (John Lindley)
  • The Thin Red Line (John Toll)

Best Film Editing

  • Saving Private Ryan (Michael Kahn)

Also Nominated

  • Out of Sight (Anne V. Coats)
  • Pleasantville (William Goldenberg)
  • The Truman Show (William M. Anderson and Lee Smith)

Best Foreign Language Film

  • Life is Beautiful

Also Nominated

  • The Celebration
  • Central Station

Best Documentary

  • The Big One

Also Nominated

  • The Cruise
  • Wild Man Blues
